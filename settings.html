AI Prompts

These prompts are what gets copied to your clipboard when you click the Cover Letter Prompt or Answer Question Prompt buttons. You can edit them below, and when you click them, the {{ page_content }} tag in the prompt text will be replaced with the text from the webage you are on and the {{ buttons_info }} tag will be replaced with the info you have entered on the buttons above. You can add more info to these like copy paste in information from your CV and tell the AI that it can use that as well, or maybe provide a model cover letter you have written and are happy with as an example for it to use.
